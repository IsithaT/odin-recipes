Coffee

What is coffee

Coffee is the miricle drink that fuels society and keeps humankind moving forward. There are many ways to make coffee; pour over, cappuccino, french press, aeropress, moka pot, chemex, etc. However the ingredients used are the same

Ingredients

  • Coffee beans or preground coffee)
  • hot water

Steps

  1. grind coffee (you can skip this step if you bought pre-ground)
  2. infuse with water
  3. add sugar or milk if you want
